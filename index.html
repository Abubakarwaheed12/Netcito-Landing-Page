What’s a common agency owner that “fits the profile” of a great agencito member?

Membership is by application only. You might be a fit if you are:

check_circle

Participating in the Agency Builder training program

check_circle

Running a successful agency with at least 2-3 people on your team

check_circle

See the challenges of entrepreneurship as opportunities to grow as a leader and human being

check_circle

Interested in learning from and contributing to other agency owners

check_circle

Are committed to bracing equity and inclusion in your life and career

check_circle

Available to commit to 3-hour meeting once per month and devote time between meetings to working through Agency Builder coursework

Agencito is probably not a good fit if you are:

check_circle

Still *want to be* working primarily as an individual contributor

This program is designed to get you OUT of the business, installing operational systems, support, and resources.

check_circle

Fully satisfied with your current volume of business and prospects for growth

Agencito is for entrepreneurs who want to be held accountable to growing their businesses revenue, team, and profit.

check_circle

Unwilling to share your professional and personal challenges with a trusted group of peers.

Agencito is about going further together. This is a partnership and only works if we’re all rowing the boat together, in the same direction.
